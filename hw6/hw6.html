Homework 5: Census Data Choropleth

Kat Anderson

This choropleth shows a 5 year estimate of median income over 12 months for the counties in Montana. It was collected by the American Community Survey from 2017 to 2021, and is now accessible on the U.S. Census Bureau website. I chose to use median to visualize income distribution instead of mean so that the data would not be skewed by outliers.
